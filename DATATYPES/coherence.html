Statistical estimates for MT Transfer Functions

Coherency: COH

 

For any two measurements A and B (in the temporal frequency domain), the coherency between A and B is defined as:

COH=\frac{| \langle A B^* \rangle |^2}{ \langle A A^* \rangle \langle B B^* \rangle }

A and B are commonly the orthogonal electric and magnetic fields.
