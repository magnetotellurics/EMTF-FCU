Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

MT Tipper Magnitude: TIPMAG

 

The tipper magnitude can be defined as:

TIPMAG=sqrt \bigg( {|T_x|^2+|T_y|^2 \bigg)

where Tx and Ty are tipper components.
