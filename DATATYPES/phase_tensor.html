Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

Phase Tensor: PT

 

Phase tensor is defined by the 4 angles, computed from 4 impedance components Zxx, Zxy, Zyx, Zyy as:

\begin{bmatrix} \phi_{xx} & \phi_{xy}\\ \phi_{yx} & \phi_{yy} \end{bmatrix}

See also Impedance Phase .
