Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

MT Induction Arrow Angle (Parkinson convention): INDANG

 

The Induction Arrow is a graphical representation of tipper T. The real and imaginary parts of complex tipper components Tx,Ty can be combined to form +/- (Txr,Tyr) and +/- (Txi,Tyi). In Parkinson convention -(Txr,Tyr) is chosen as Inductioon Arrow, which tends to point toward regions of higher conductivity. Thus Induction Arrow angle in degrees can be defined as:

INDANG=atan \bigg( \frac{T_{yr}}{T_{xr}} \bigg)+180^o
