Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

Inter-station magnetic field transfer functions: P

 

Vertical Field Transfer Functions (Tipper) relate the local vertical magnetic field component Hz to the two local horizontal magnetic field components Hx, Hy. The complex tipper components, Tx and Ty, satisfy the tipper relation:

H_z = T_x H_x + T_y H_y

An inter-station MT tipper is a variant of the MT tipper, where the magnetic field $H_x$ and $H_y$ is obtained at a remote site,

H_z_1 = P_x H_x_2 + P_y H_y_2

where indices 1 and 2 indicate two different site locations.

See also MT tipper and interstation MT impedance.
