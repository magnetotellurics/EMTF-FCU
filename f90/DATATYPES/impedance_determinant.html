MT Impedance Determinant: DET

 

Determinant of MT impedance is invariant under rotation:

 

DET= |\begin{bmatrix} Z_{xx} & Z_{xy}\\ Z_{yx} & Z_{yy} \end{bmatrix} |=Z_{xx}*Z_{yy}-Z_{yx}*Z_{xy}

where Zxx , Zxy , Zyx , Zyy are 4 components of MT impedance tenzor.
