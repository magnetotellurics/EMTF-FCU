Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

MT Impedance Skew: ZSCEW

 

The impedance skew is usually defined as:

 

ZSCEW=\frac{|Z'_{xx}+Z'_{yy}|}{|Z'_{xy}-Z'_{yx}|}

where Z'xx , Z'xy , Z'yx , and Z'yy are the rotated impedance components.
