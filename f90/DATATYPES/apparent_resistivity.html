Statistical estimates and definitions for MT Transfer Functions

Apparent resistivity: RHO

 

Apparent resistivity is computed from MT impedance as:

\rho_i_j= \frac{|Z_i_j|^2}{\omega\mu} \hspace{1cm} i,j=x,y \\ \text {where } \mu \text { is the permeability of free space, } \omega \text { is the frequency in radians. }

Zxy are components of surface impedance tensor.
Apparent resistivity units are [Ohm m].
