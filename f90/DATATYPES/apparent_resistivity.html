Statistical estimates and definitions for MT Transfer Functions

Apparent resistivities: RHO

 

Apparent resistivities are given as:

\rho_i_j= \frac{|Z_i_j|^2}{\omega\mu} \hspace{1cm} i,j=x,y \\ \text {where } \mu \text { is the permeability of free space, } \omega \text { is the frequency in radians, }\\ Z_x_y \text{ are components of surface impedance tensor.}\\ \rho_i_j \text { are in units of W-meters.}
