Statistical estimates and definitions for MT Transfer Functions

MT Off Diagonal Impedance: Z

 

Only off-diagonal elements (Zxy and Zyx) of the impedance tensor:

\begin{pmatrix} E_x\\ E_y \end{pmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} Z_{xx} & Z_{xy}\\ Z_{yx} & Z_{yy} \end{bmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} H_x\\ H_y \end{pmatrix}

There is an inherent 90 degree ambiguity in the calculated principal directions which can only be resolved by interpreting the off-diagonal element ( Zxy or Zyx ) of the rotated impedance tensor best represents the E-parallel (TE) mode and which the E-perpendicular(TM) mode.
