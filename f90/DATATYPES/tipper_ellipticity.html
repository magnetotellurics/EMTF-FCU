Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

MT Tipper Ellipticity: TELLIP

 

The tipper ellipticity is usually defined as:

TELLIP=\frac{|(T_x-T_y)*cos(2B)+(T_x+T_y)*sin(2B)|}{|(T_x+T_y)*cos(2B)+(T_x-Z_y)*sin(2B)|}

where Tx and Ty - are tipper components, and B is the calculated tipper principal direction.
