Definitions for MT Transfer Functions and Derived Quantities

MT Tipper Skew: TSCEW

 

The tipper skew is usually defined as:

 

TSCEW=\frac{|T'_x+T'_y|}{|T'_x-T'_y|}

where T'x and T'y are the rotated tipper components.
