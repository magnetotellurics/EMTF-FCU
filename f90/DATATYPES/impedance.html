Statistical estimates and definitions for MT Transfer Functions

MT Impedance: Z

 

MT impedance is a characteristic measure of the EM properties of the sub surface medium, and constitutes the basic MT Transfer function. The surface impedance tensor has four components: Zxx, Zxy, Zyx and Zyy, which satisfy the impedance relations:

 

\begin{pmatrix} E_x\\ E_y \end{pmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} Z_{xx} & Z_{xy}\\ Z_{yx} & Z_{yy} \end{bmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} H_x\\ H_y \end{pmatrix}

Impedance units are [mV/km]/[nT].
